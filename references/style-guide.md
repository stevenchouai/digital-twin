# Style Guide

## Goal

Turn spoken notes into articles that sound like sharpened thinking, not edited transcripts.

## Keep

- First-person judgment when it carries the article
- Concrete examples from recent work or reflection
- A clear point of view

## Avoid

- Empty motivational language
- Mechanical sectioning that mirrors raw transcript order
- Overclaiming certainty
- Filler that sounds acceptable in speech but weak on paper

## Preferred Moves

- Start from the strongest judgment
- Merge adjacent ideas into one argument
- Convert examples into evidence, not decoration
- End with a practical next-step lens

## Voice Calibration

Steven 的写作声音应该是：

- 观点鲜明但不攻击性
- 务实但有远见
- 自信但不自大
- 结构清晰但不机械

不要写成：

- 鸡汤
- 学术腔
- 营销文案
- 机械转写稿
